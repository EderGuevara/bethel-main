Who We Are

More Than an Agency —
A Movement

Bethel Financial Group is a life insurance agency committed to protecting families and building the next generation of financial professionals. We believe in developing people, not just policies.

Based across MA, NY, FL, NJ, and MD, our team of licensed agents delivers personalized financial protection solutions while building meaningful careers in the insurance industry.

Services Built Around Your Needs

From protecting your family to building your career, Bethel Financial Group provides the products and support you need at every stage.

🏠
Life Insurance

Term, whole life, universal life, and more. We help families find the right coverage to protect what matters most — at every stage of life.

📈
Financial Planning

Retirement planning, annuities, and wealth protection strategies tailored to your goals. Build a financial future that lasts generations.

🎓
Agent Training

Full support from pre-licensing through your first sale. Our training program gives new agents the tools, knowledge, and confidence to succeed.

👥
Team Building

Business Insurance

🤝
Client Support

We don't disappear after the sale. Ongoing policy reviews, beneficiary updates, and dedicated agent support ensure your coverage always fits your life.
