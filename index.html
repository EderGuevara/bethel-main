Who We Are

More Than an Agency —
A Movement

Bethel Financial Group is a life insurance agency committed to protecting families and building the next generation of financial professionals. We believe in developing people, not just policies.

Based across MA, NY, FL, NJ, and MD, our team of licensed agents delivers personalized financial protection solutions while building meaningful careers in the insurance industry.
